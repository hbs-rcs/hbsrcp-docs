## Using Launchers 

Upon logging in, you will see tiles with all projects that you have access to:

### Selecting and Starting Launchers
Click on your project and select one of the available **launchers**. Note that each launcher has multiple configurations of CPUs/GPUs and memory, and that these vary by [software](software.md).
<img width="932" height="245" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9df0bebc-9f24-4562-9b83-acfd03d62bd2" />

Select your software, name the launcher, and choose the CPU/GPU and memory allocation that is most appropriate for your work. Keep in mind that there are different [costs](/hbsrcp-docs/billing/) associated with each configuration. **Starting a launcher for the first time can take 5-10 minutes.**

<img width="368" height="335" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/35ca678b-9f37-4c74-a731-76be37e43a13" />

Once your launcher has been provisioned, click on "Start" to open the software in your browser:
<img width="288" height="181" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c4c035dd-33cb-4409-9a90-041ddb06c7c4" />


### Stopping and Terminating Launchers
If you have finished with your session, but plan to return to it, please ensure you have **stopped** it. Keeping the launcher running, even if you are not actively using it, will incur charges.
